A friendly and artistic, weather and air-quality index guide in form of a mobile application.

Overview

WeatherProof is a mobile application that shows weather conditions along with air-quality index and gives you friendly suggestions to keep you protected. The application is specifically useful in places where the air quality is degrading day-by-day, like my hometown Delhi, and often reaches hazardous levels. The application is different form normal weather apps as it gives the proper suggestions according the to both the air quality and the weather conditions.
